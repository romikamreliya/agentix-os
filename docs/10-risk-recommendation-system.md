# Risk & Recommendation System

## Overview

The Risk & Recommendation System helps Agentix OS identify problems before they occur and discover opportunities for improvement.

Risks and recommendations are first-class entities with their own lifecycle, governance, traceability, and approval workflows.

The system enables proactive decision-making rather than reactive problem-solving.

---

# Purpose

The Risk & Recommendation System exists to:

* Identify project risks early
* Reduce project failures
* Improve project quality
* Provide actionable recommendations
* Support governance
* Improve future outcomes
* Enable continuous improvement

---

# Core Principles

## Risks Are First-Class Entities

Risks are not stored only inside reviews.

Risks have their own:

* Lifecycle
* Status
* Severity
* Ownership
* History

---

## Recommendations Are First-Class Entities

Recommendations are independent entities.

Recommendations can originate from:

* Reviews
* Research
* Agents
* Knowledge
* Risk Analysis

---

## AI Recommends, Humans Decide

Recommendations never create actions automatically.

Users decide whether to:

* Approve
* Reject
* Convert into tasks
* Convert into knowledge
* Convert into blueprint improvements
