# Review & Approval System

## Overview

The Review & Approval System provides governance, quality control, traceability, and human oversight throughout Agentix OS.

This system ensures that important decisions, deliverables, improvements, and project milestones are evaluated before being accepted.

Reviews evaluate quality.

Approvals authorize action.

Both are independent systems that work together.

---

# Purpose

The Review & Approval System exists to:

* Maintain quality standards
* Reduce project risks
* Improve decision-making
* Provide governance
* Enable traceability
* Support continuous improvement

---

# Core Principles

## Review Before Approval

Approvals should be informed.

Recommended workflow:

```text
Work Completed
      ↓
Review
      ↓
Risk Analysis
      ↓
Approval
```

Approvals should not occur without sufficient review.

---

## AI Recommends, Humans Decide

AI agents may:

* Analyze
* Review
* Recommend

Users remain responsible for approvals.

---

## Traceability Everywhere

Every review and approval should answer:

```text
What was reviewed?

Who reviewed it?

What was found?

Why was it approved?
```

# Review System

## What Is a Review?

A review is an evaluation of an entity.

Reviews identify:

* Issues
* Risks
* Opportunities
* Recommendations

Reviews do not make final decisions.

---
